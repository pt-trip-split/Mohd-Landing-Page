Trip Split App

Trip Split allows traveling buddies to record bills on the app, split bills, and request money from frineds and family for whom they paid.

App Features

Add Friens

Add your travel buddies and creates groups and destinations where you are traveling

Pay Bills

Pay bills at restaurants etc. and record what you paid in the app, so it's visible to everyone.

Split Bills

Split the bills per person as percentage or fixed dollar values what each person owes you

Receive Payments

Request the person to pay you and you can receive the money in your bank or paypal
